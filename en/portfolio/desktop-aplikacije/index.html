Desktop Applications

In the time when internet is widespread, developing a desktop application may sound like a total waste of time, right? On the contrary, the need for this type of application is still large - most of the companies with closed/private systems are still in massive demand for applications that are in no way exposed to the internet. We at Tech9320 have recognized these needs, and have trained our team to develop high-quality and modern-looking desktop applications, in order to meet the expectations and needs of our clients.

Technologies

Considering that we have the most knowledge in JavaScript programing language, for developing desktop applications we mostly use Electron, the most famous JavaScript framework for developing these types of applications. It enables a very fast development, with workflow and visual experience very similar to classical web applications. Of course, there are a lot of other alternatives. In the world of JS that’s Tauri, besides him, in our case, that would be C# with .NET framework.Everything is of course determined by the needs of the clients, and we try to be as flexible as we can during the development.

Workflow

Tech9320 is a team - and the whole work is based on team spirit and mutual cooperation. Steps for creating a desktop application are simple

  1. Planning

Consists of designing user interface considering demands made by the client, as well as the plan for the whole workflow, adapted by the deadline set by the client.

  1. Development

Classical programming work, which consists of writing clean and sustainable code. Tasks are clearly divided into sections, and every team member is responsible for their own, and others parts. Our goal is that the product we are creating should be on the highest level which will satisfy even the toughest clients

  1. Testing

Before we give project a green light, the whole system should be tested - is everything functioning as expected. Through the whole process of development and testing, we are in constant contact with the client, because we really care about your opinions throughout the whole process.

  1. Final touches

The application will be packed and sent to you exactly the way you want it. Any other modifications and maintaining services can be negotiated

Price

The price is always negotiable. Depending on the size of the project, time limit, purpose of the application, and many other tiny variables, price could go up or down. Agreement is the fundamental building block for good relationship, so why not use it to build an amazing application as well!
