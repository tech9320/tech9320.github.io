Supply Chain Forum

Recently, we had the opportunity to present our project, Agroinvest, at the Supply Chain Forum as Tech9320, which was a great honor for us. Agroinvest is one of our newest projects that aims to use open data for analyzing and predicting trends in the agricultural sector to make better investment decisions.

The project originated from the Egzakta Open Data competition, and we invested a lot of time in it. It is intended for anyone who would like to get involved in agriculture and invest in it, as well as for those who already have a part of their portfolio in agriculture and would like to take it to the next level.

During the event, we had the opportunity to meet many experts and companies from the industry, both agriculture and IT, and we took the opportunity to talk and exchange ideas.

We would like to thank everyone who gave us the opportunity to present our project and our team. We hope to have the opportunity to collaborate and contribute to the development of our and your ideas in the future, and to grow and develop together.

Thank you for reading this blog post and following our team on our journey. Stay with us to learn more about our projects and industry news. We are preparing to launch new projects soon that you don’t want to miss.
